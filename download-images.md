## How to Download:

1. Click on the Unsplash link for each dish
2. Find a good quality image
3. Click "Download free" button
4. Save the image with the exact filename shown above
5. Place all images in the `food-ordering-website/images/` folder

## Alternative: Use Your Own Images

If you have your own food photos:
1. Take high-quality photos of your dishes
2. Resize them to 800x600px
3. Save with the filenames above
4. Place in the `images/` folder
